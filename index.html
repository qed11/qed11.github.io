Welcome to my webpage. This site is used to host some of my open-source resources and notes, and is currently under construction.

I am an Aerospace Engineering student studying at the University of Toronto. My primary interest lies in development of numerical methods for aerospace applications and optimization, specifically for hypersonic vehicles and motor sports.

I worked at SpaceRyde as a propulsion intern from Sept, 2022 to Feb, 2023, designing Canada's first orbital-class rocket.

I previously worked in the 2022 summer with Prof. Masayuki Yano from ACE Lab at UTIAS, learning about numerical methods and optimization schemes for airfoil geometry optimization.

I have also previously worked at Légère Reeds lead by Prof. Mark Kortschot, as a design engineer intern for 2 summers in 2020 and 2021, where I worked on optimizing of the design process of synthetic instrument reeds.

I dream to become an educator who shapes students dreams, and I have been teaching physics for Grade 11 and 12 students at Perfect Education.

Apart from academia, I am an Oboist/English Hornist. I have previously served as the oboist in the Toronto Symphony Youth Orchestra, and am currently studying with Hugo Lee from the Toronto Symphony Orchestra.
