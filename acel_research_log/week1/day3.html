  1. The derivative of the lagrange basis used in a linear interpolation is exactly the segment length. The lagrange basis is defined in the AER336 notes. On segment $K_i \equiv [x_{i-1}, x_{i}]$, the lagrange basis polynomial is given as $$l_i(x) \equiv \frac{x - x_{i-1}}{x_{i} - x_{i-1}}$$ Taking the derivative with respect to $x$, we get $$\frac{dl_i}{dx} = \frac{1}{x_i - x_{i-1}} = \frac{1}{h_i}$$
