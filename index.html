Welcome to my webpage. This site is used to host some of my open-source resources and notes, and is currently under construction.

I am an Aerospace Engineering student studying at University of Toronto. My primary interest lies in the development and applications of civil hypersonic vehicles, particularly in the aerodynamic design and propulsion system optimizaitons.

This summer, I am working with Prof. Masayuki Yano from Aerospace Computational Engineering Lab here in University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies, developing adaptive mesh refinement algorithms for aerodynamic shape optimization. Afterwards, I will be joining SpaceRyde, starting from September of 2022, as a propulsion intern. I have previously worked at Légère Reeds lead by Prof. Mark Kortshot as a design engineer intern, working on optimizing design process of synthetic instrument reeds.
